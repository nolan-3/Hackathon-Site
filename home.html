Beginners Welcome!

High Schoolers!

All Skill Levels Welcome!

Whether you've been coding for years or you've never written a line of code, you're still absolutely encouraged to come. We'll hold beginner workshops and help you along the way to make sure you have an amazing experience.

Totally Free!

Everything at this event is totally free, including meals, drinks, swag, and prizes. All you'll need to bring is a laptop; if you don't have one, contact us and we'll figure something out.



What to Expect

Our hackathon is a 10-hour coding event that brings together interested students to collaborate and build amazing projects. Here are some of the key features of the event:

Learning

There will be lots of opportunities to learn from those around you, and you can participate in our workshops to learn important programming skills

Competition and Swag

Compete against other teams and individuals to build the best project. Prizes will be awarded to the best projects. There will also be free swag such as shirts, water bottles, and stickers

Fun

You'll have the chance to meet with other interested and aspiring hackers, and build relationships, and you'll just get to play around in some organized games.
