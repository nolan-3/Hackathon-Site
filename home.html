What to Expect

Our hackathon is a 10-hour coding event that brings together interested and talented students to collaborate and build amazing projects. Here are some of the key features of the event:

Learn

There will be lots of opportunities to learn from those around you, and you can participate in our workshops to learn important programming skills

Competition and Swag

Compete against other teams and individuals to build the best project. Prizes will be awarded to the best projects. There will also be free swag such as shirts, water bottles, and stickers

Have Fun

You'll have the chance to meet with other interested and aspiring hackers, and build relationships. You'll get to play around in our organized games.
